Cold Brewed Iced Tea

This cold brewed method creates a smooth tea without any bitterness. Feel free to sweeten with honey or a simple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 6 - 8 tea bags depending on how strong you'd like it
  • 4 cups of filtered water

Steps

  1. Combine tea and water in a quart sized mason jar, and cover
  2. Place jar in the fridge for at least 12 hours
  3. When it is done brewing, remove tea bags and sweeten to taste
