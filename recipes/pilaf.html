InstantPot Pilaf

Quick and Tasty Sidedish with Pantry Staples

This tasty sidedish has less than 10 ingredients, and you probably have them in the cupboard already. It tastes like a homemade version of Rice-a-Roni.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs of Butter
  • 2 oz of orzo pasta, or spaghetti broken into small pieces
  • 1 cup of long grain rice
  • 1.25 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion flakes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric

Steps

  1. Using InstantPot's saute function, melt butter and add pasta
  2. Stir frequently until pasta just starts to turn brown
  3. Add rice, stir and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Pour a little bit of the broth into the hot pot, and scrape up any browned bits on the bottom
  5. Turn of saute, and add the remaining broth
  6. Stir in spices
  7. Put on lid, seal vent, and set pot to manual cook for 10 minutes
  8. When the cooktime finishes, let the pot naturally release pressure for 10 minutes, and then quick release the remaining pressure
  9. Fluff rice and enjoy!
