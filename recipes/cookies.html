Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Everyone has tried them. Everyone loves them.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Whisk together dry ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside
  3. In a large bowl beat butter and sugar together until fluffy
  4. Add eggs one at a time, stirring between each one
  5. Add vanilla extract, stir
  6. Add dry ingredients a in three smaller batches, stirring to combine after each addition
  7. Stir in chocolate chips
  8. Scoop tablespoon sized balls of dough onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes
